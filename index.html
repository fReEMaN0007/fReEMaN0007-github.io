On Call Computer Technician
This job helps me enhance my critical thinking skills, analytical skills, and negotiation skills. My first computer was a desktop PC with an Intel Pentium 3 800 MHz processor, 512 MB of RAM, and 60 GB of storage. During that time, a PC with those kinds of specs was already expensive. And more expensive if you don't have any knowledge of computer troubleshooting and repair. So I asked my father to buy me a book. A book I saw in a National Book Store in Cubao. That book contains everything you need to know about computers (parts, assembly, and troubleshooting). So every time my PC has a problem, I am able to fix it. And that's how I became an on-call computer technician. It started with friends and family, then referrals kept coming, and I started working with different people and solving different computer problems.
Lights & Sound Tech
This job helps me enhance my interpersonal and leadership skills. I became a lights and sound tech from 2010 to 2015. During my time as a lights and sound tech, I handled an entry-level lights and sound system with 5 to 10 crew members. I'm mainly in charge of handling the audio mixer. I also handle equipment safety, staff training, technical know-how, and sometimes client meetings.
Printing Shop Owner
This job helps me enhance my creativity, time management, customer relations skills, and managerial skills. With the help of my former employer, I was able to put up a small printing business from 2015–2017, but it only lasted for 2 years because I opted to finish my studies. My main product is digital printing, and then I learned the skill of screen printing. I mostly do everything from scratch. Clients tell me their ideas, and I help them come up with a design using graphic tools. This job helps me value time and make the most of any resources available. I learned a lot from this job. Some of my clients are business owners too; others are freelancers, so I was able to learn a lot about other industries like garments, party needs, water refilling, photo and video, food, food processing, etc.
